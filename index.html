🌅 Sunrise / Sunset hike checklist
Essential gear
🔦 Headlamp with fresh batteries — you will be hiking in the dark
🧥 Warm layers — the summit is significantly colder than the base
💧 2+ liters of water — more than you think you need
🍫 Snacks — you will be out for several hours total
📱 Fully charged phone — for safety and photos
👟 Proper footwear — trail shoes or hiking boots, no flip flops
🕶️ Sunglasses and sunscreen — sun rises fast at altitude
🩹 Basic first aid — blister pads especially
Sunrise specific
⏰ Set multiple alarms — missing your departure time means missing the sunrise
🌡️ Check weather the night before — temperatures drop sharply before dawn
🅿️ Arrive at parking early — lots fill fast even before sunrise
📷 Camera or tripod if you want shots — the light window is short
🌬️ Wind at the summit can be intense — a windbreaker is worth it
